# RSOLV Early Access Support Channels

Welcome to the RSOLV Early Access Support program! As an early access user, you have several dedicated support channels to help ensure your success with RSOLV.

## Available Support Channels

### Email Support

Our primary support channel for all technical issues, questions, and feedback.

- **Technical Support**: support@rsolv.dev
- **Early Access Program**: early-access@rsolv.dev
- **Security Concerns**: security@rsolv.dev
- **Billing Questions**: billing@rsolv.dev

**Response SLA**: All emails will receive an initial response within 4 business hours during Early Access.

### Discord Community

Join our private Discord server for real-time support, discussions with other early access users, and direct access to the RSOLV development team.

- **Invitation Link**: You'll receive a personal invitation in your welcome email
- **Channels**:
  - `#introductions`: Introduce yourself to the community
  - `#general`: General discussion about RSOLV
  - `#technical-support`: Technical questions and troubleshooting
  - `#feature-requests`: Suggest new features and improvements
  - `#showcase`: Share your success stories and examples
  - `#announcements`: Official announcements from the RSOLV team

## How to Report Issues

When reporting issues, please include:

1. **Issue Description**: Clear description of the problem
2. **Steps to Reproduce**: Detailed steps to reproduce the issue
3. **Expected vs. Actual Behavior**: What you expected vs. what happened
4. **Environment Details**:
   - Repository size and structure
   - Language(s) in use
   - GitHub Action configuration
   - Error messages or logs
5. **Impact**: How this is affecting your work

## Feedback and Feature Requests

Your feedback is invaluable during this early access phase. To provide structured feedback:

1. **Use the Feedback Form**: [rsolv.dev/feedback](https://rsolv.dev/feedback)
2. **GitHub Discussions**: Post in the Ideas & Feature Requests category
3. **Direct Email**: Send detailed feedback to early-access@rsolv.dev

We prioritize feature development based on early access user feedback, so please be specific about how RSOLV can better serve your needs.

## Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability:

1. **DO NOT** post it publicly in GitHub Discussions or Discord
2. Email security@rsolv.dev immediately with details
3. Encrypt sensitive information using our [PGP key](https://rsolv.dev/pgp-key.txt)

## Support Escalation

If you're not receiving timely or satisfactory support:

1. Email escalations@rsolv.dev with your case details
2. Include "ESCALATION" in the subject line
3. Provide reference to previous communication
